Akan Names Project

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were
and the order in which they were born.
These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.
Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.
This project will help in taking in the user's birth records and outputs the name in accordance to the day of the week
he/she was born.
